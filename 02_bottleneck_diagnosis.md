
PART 1: Bottleneck Diagnosis Using Prompted AI Dialogue
Prompt 1: Founder Mindset Layer

 Prompt

“Analyze the founder transcript and identify patterns indicating mindset constraints around control, delegation, and decision ownership. Highlight implicit fears or beliefs limiting scale.”

What this uncovers

Fear of loss of control

Identity tied to being the bottleneck

Delegation without true empowerment

Cialdini Principle Used

Consistency – revealing gaps between founder’s stated desire to scale and actual behaviors

Negative Prompt

“Avoid psychoanalyzing beyond evidence present in the transcript.”

Likely Problem Layer
✅ Founder’s mental model

Prompt 2: Organizational / Systemic Layer

 Prompt

“Map operational breakdowns implied in the transcript (decision latency, unclear ownership, quality issues). Identify missing systems or rituals.”

What this uncovers

Role ambiguity

Lack of decision frameworks

No operating cadence

Cialdini Principle Used

Authority – framing diagnosis through proven operating systems

Negative Prompt

“Do not recommend generic best practices without linking to transcript signals.”

Likely Problem Layer
✅ Talent / process capability

Prompt 3: Market & Capability Alignment

 Prompt

“Evaluate market positioning, pricing discipline, and customer selection based on the transcript. Identify strategic drift.”

What this uncovers

Referral-led, reactive GTM

Inconsistent margins

Absence of ICP clarity

Cialdini Principle Used

Social Proof – benchmarking against structured B2B growth patterns

Negative Prompt

“Avoid assuming competitive landscape details not mentioned.”
